Who I Am

With over 15 years of experience in Long Island real estate, I've developed a deep understanding of the Nassau and Suffolk County markets. I've helped hundreds of families buy, sell, and invest in properties they love.

My approach is simple: put your goals first. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a growing family, or an experienced investor, I listen, advise, and execute with precision and transparency.

I believe real estate transactions should feel personal, not transactional. That's why I treat every client like family and stay available throughout the entire process.
